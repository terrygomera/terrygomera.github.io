GUIDELINES FOR SUBMISSIONS OF ABSTRACTS

Abstracts of 250-300 words (for papers) and 300-400 words (for panels) should be submitted by e-mail as MS Word files to:

  • Conference Secretariat: (insert email address)
  • Dr Raymond Taruvinga: raytaruvinga@africau.edu
  • Mr Gondayi Mzite: mziteg@africau.edu

Paper and Panel Abstracts should be accompanied by the following information:

  • Title, full name(s), and surname(s) of author(s)
  • Institutional affiliation (i.e., position of the delegate, institution’s name, faculty/school, department, and location)
  • E-mail address (for *panels and papers with multiple authors, please state name and email of corresponding author)
  • Contact details (mobile and/or office number of corresponding author/panelist)
  • E-mail address (for *panels and papers with multiple authors, please state name and email of corresponding author)
  • Full title of the paper
  • Abstract
  • Preferred conference sub-theme

* A panel is an opportunity for a group of interested delegates to explore a topic and share innovative research on an aspect of the Conference theme. A panel should have at least four (4) authors

Kindly note that the language of the conference will be English.
